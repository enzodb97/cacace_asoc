About Us

Origin

The Cacace & Asociados study, with its headquarters in the city of Rosario, was founded by Dr Luciano Esteban Cacace. It currently operates in the provinces of Santa Fe, Buenos Aires, Neuquén and Río Negro, with an evolutionary and developmental tendency in the legal world. C&A is in constant search to provide fast and efficient solutions when facing conflicts of interests.

It does not limit its actions to the region of Santa Fe but through its correspondents and associate colleagues it extents its actions to several provinces of the country, those are: Buenos aires, Cordoba, Entre Rios and Rio Negro. At the same time, its vinculation with studios in Montevideo, New York, Montreal and Rome allow the resolution of conflicts and the concretion of bussiness and international transactions.

Links

Institucional links of our legal study

  • AIDA Association Internationale du Droit des Assurances (A.I.D.A)

    Aida Argentina: The ARGENTINE ASSOCIATION OF INSURANCE LAW has as an objective the study and divulgation of all manners related to Insurance Law, being them of any genre or species, favoring and developing international collaboration to this matter. Feedback from this honorable asociation implies an inexorable link to insurance career’s professional development.

  • Protection and Indemnity Association

    P&I: The Protection and Indemnity Associations, known as P&I Clubs, or also Pandi Clubs are entities made up of shipowners, which, in a mutual system, provide marine insurance coverage in relation to all those civil liability risks that are not covered by the policies of helmet and machinery.

  • Argentine Catholic University (ACU)

    Understanding that education is the main path for constant development, we maintain an intership regime with the Argentine Catholic University. By doing so, training students can start their development in the legal world by staying in natural evolution.

  • Chamber of Deputies of the Province of Santa Fe

    As advisers to the MERCOSUR Regional Integration Commission and Paraguay-Paraná Waterway of the Chamber of Deputies of the Province of Santa Fe, we maintain relationships with our legislators interested in the current and social future of our province and nation.

  • Portico Real Estate- International FIABICI

    Our client and collaborator, Portico Real Estate has alowed a nexus with the main international institute of Real Estate with active participation in the United Nations. Its relationships and operations are viscerally regulated by a strict code of ethics and professionalism.

  • Union of Occupational Risk Insurers

    Union of Occupational Risk Insurers: The exchange of ideas and management policies between insurances groups is essential when coordinating a development path.

  • Federation of the Argentine Naval Industry

    The Federation of the Argentine Naval Industry was in fact established as an association around 1962, by the will of two pre-existing associations: the Union of Naval Builders and the Association of Argentine Shipyards and Naval Workshops. Its purpose is to promote the development, protection and stimulation of the Naval industry and to defend its interests.

  • Santa Fe’s Chamber of the Naval Industry

    The Santa Fe’s Chamber of the Naval Industry was founded with the purpose of exercising the sectoral presentation with exercise the professional association of shipyards, shipbuilders, naval workshops and others related to the naval industry of the province of Santa Fe and surrounding regions, in issues linked directly or indirectly to the defense of their rights and interests.

  • Austral Argentina University

    Association of Law Graduates of Austral University.

  • Regional Forum Rosario (FRR)

    Our study actively participates in the Forum. It is a non-profit civil association whose mission is to promote "Regional Development" in economic, social, and cultural aspects to improve the quality of life in our community.
