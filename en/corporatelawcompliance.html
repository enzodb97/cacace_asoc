Corporate Law - Compliance

Notwithstanding the specific areas of law, all our proffesionals are highly qualified in the corporate world, providing the adequate support of our clients´ daily operations, as well as working in a coordinated manner and in harmony with their internal legal departments. Logically, this area implies a deep assistance in matters of Corporate Law, advising our clients in constitution, development and liquidation of Commercial Companies as well as in the necessary registration of those for their correct activity. We seek active participation in the resolution of corporate conflicts and in advising on the ideal type of figure for each legal relationship.
